# Fixture 2: Medical Treatment Protocol with Exceptions

All patients require informed consent before treatment. Patients under 18 are exempt from consent requirements. Emergency cases do not require consent. Life-threatening situations require parental consent except when parents are unavailable.

Blood pressure must be monitored every 4 hours. Stable patients need monitoring every 8 hours only. Critical patients require continuous monitoring unless contraindicated by patient allergies.

Medication dosage is 10mg per day. Elderly patients receive 5mg per day. Patients over 65 take the standard 10mg dose. Renal impairment requires 5mg dosing except in dialysis patients who take 15mg.

Treatment continues for 14 days minimum. Severe cases extend to 21 days. All cases conclude after 14 days unless complications develop. Complications require immediate discontinuation. Complications necessitate extended treatment beyond 21 days.

Side effects must be reported within 24 hours. Non-serious effects may be reported within 7 days. Serious effects require immediate reporting except for expected reactions listed in section 12. All effects must be documented regardless of severity.
