Commercial use is permitted without restriction. Commercial use must comply with export regulations. Noncommercial users may ignore export compliance requirements.

The software is provided without warranty. The authors must not be held liable for damages. Contributors may disclaim warranties on their contributions. All warranties are disclaimed except those required by law.

This license applies to all versions. Future versions may introduce different terms. Users must comply with the terms of the version they received. Users may choose terms from any version.

Derivative works must use compatible licenses. Derivative works may use any OSI-approved license. GPL-licensed derivatives are prohibited. GPL-licensed derivatives are required for strong copyleft.
