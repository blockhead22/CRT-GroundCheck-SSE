# Fixture 3: SLA with Numeric Contradictions

Response time must not exceed 4 hours during business hours. Critical incidents require response within 2 hours. All incidents receive response within 6 hours maximum. P1 incidents guarantee 1 hour response time. Standard support provides 8 hour response windows.

System uptime shall be at least 99.9% annually. Monthly uptime targets are 99.5%. The service guarantees 99.99% uptime per quarter. Acceptable downtime is no more than 8 hours per year. Planned maintenance allows 12 hours downtime monthly.

Data retention period is 90 days minimum. Archived data persists for 30 days only. Compliance requires 7 year retention. Logs must be kept for at least 365 days. Debug logs are purged after 48 hours. All transaction records require 5 year storage.

Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase. Premium customers have 60 day refund windows. The refund period expires after 14 days for digital goods. Annual subscriptions allow 90 day refund requests. All refunds require submission within 7 business days.

Minimum order quantity is 100 units. Bulk orders start at 500 units. Enterprise customers may order as few as 50 units. Sample orders require 10 unit minimum. The minimum purchasable quantity is 250 units. Trial orders accept 5 unit minimums.
