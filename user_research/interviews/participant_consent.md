## Appendix: Additional Details

### What Happens to Your Data?

1. **During Interview:**
   - Audio recording and/or notes taken
   - No personally identifiable information linked to responses beyond this consent form

2. **After Interview:**
   - Recording transcribed (by researcher or transcription service with confidentiality agreement)
   - Transcript anonymized (all identifying info removed)
   - Original recording securely stored, then deleted after 90 days

3. **Analysis Phase:**
   - Anonymized transcripts analyzed for themes and patterns
   - Quotes may be used in reports (without attribution to you)
   - Quantitative metrics aggregated across all participants

4. **Reporting:**
   - Findings published in research reports
   - No individual data identifiable
   - Aggregate statistics only

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Ask questions about the research at any time
- Receive a copy of this consent form
- Withdraw from the study at any time
- Request deletion of your data (before final analysis)
- Decline to answer any question
- Stop recording at any time
- Review your transcript (upon request)

### Data Security Measures

We take the following precautions to protect your data:
- Encrypted storage of all recordings and transcripts
- Access restricted to research team only
- Secure deletion of recordings after analysis
- Anonymization before any sharing
- No linking of consent forms to transcript data in analysis
